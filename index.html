About Josh

The Beginning, Middle, and Now

The Younger Years

Long Island, NY

I grew up on Long Island, NY living with my mom, dad, and older brother. I started off as early as I can remember in sports. My brother fueled my drive for competition as my biggest rival for as long as I can remember. This strong rivalry helped me develop a great relationship with my brother, as well as my best skill.

To set a goal and work hard towards obtaining it.

College Days

Hartford, CT

While working part-time, I worked towards my goal of completing my bachelor's degree in Management. I managed to continue doing what I loved by playing sports while pursuing my degree. During the Summers, I worked over at the YMCA to help the next generation and give them an example to follow.

I finished my B.A./B.S. in Management at the University of Hartford.

Now

Long Island, NY

I am grateful for the experiences that have transformed me into who I am today. I have applied what I learned from my education and experiences through working as the Director of Sports Camp at the YMCA, and through sales at South Shore Hyundai. I can wholeheartedly say, I can't wait to see how far I go.

I have set my goal for success, I just need to do what I've always been doing, and work hard to obtain it.
